# Modal Verbs in English

## 1. Pengertian Modal Verbs
**Modal verbs** adalah kata kerja bantu (auxiliary verbs) yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan:
- kemampuan (ability)
- kemungkinan (possibility)
- izin (permission)
- keharusan (obligation)
- saran (advice)
- larangan (prohibition)

Modal verbs **tidak berubah bentuk** meskipun subjeknya berbeda dan **selalu diikuti verb dasar (bare infinitive)**.

Contoh:
- She **can** swim.
- You **must** study harder.

---

## 2. Daftar Modal Verbs Umum

| Modal | Fungsi Utama |
|------|--------------|

| might | kemungkinan kecil |

| shall | tawaran / formal future |

| will | masa depan, keputusan |

---

## 3. Penggunaan Modal Verbs

### 3.1 Can
Digunakan untuk:
- kemampuan
- izin informal

Contoh:
- I **can** speak English.
- **Can** I borrow your book?

---

### 3.2 Could
Digunakan untuk:
- kemampuan di masa lalu
- permintaan lebih sopan

Contoh:
- I **could** swim when I was five.
- **Could** you help me?

---

### 3.3 May & Might
Digunakan untuk:
- kemungkinan
- izin (may lebih formal)

Contoh:
- It **may** rain today.
- She **might** come later.
- **May** I enter the room?

---

### 3.4 Must
Digunakan untuk:
- kewajiban kuat
- aturan

Contoh:
- You **must** wear a helmet.
- Students **must** be on time.

---

### 3.5 Should
Digunakan untuk:
- saran
- rekomendasi

Contoh:
- You **should** study every day.
- We **should** leave now.

---

### 3.6 Will & Would

#### Will
Digunakan untuk:
- keputusan cepat
- masa depan

Contoh:
- I **will** call you later.
- She **will** help us.

#### Would
Digunakan untuk:
- permintaan sopan
- conditional sentence

Contoh:
- **Would** you like some tea?
- I **would** travel if I had money.
